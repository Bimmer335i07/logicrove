05 · Questions

A few things operators ask.

Who is this for?
Senior healthcare IT operators. Hospital CIOs and CMIOs, health system CISOs, imaging center IT directors, payer IT leaders, revenue cycle operators, and the VPs who run under them. If your work is to keep a health system’s IT operational and moving forward, the Brief is written for you.
How is this different from the newsletters I already get?
Most healthcare IT newsletters either reprint vendor press releases with a two sentence intro, or publish analyst commentary written by people who have never actually run a system. The Brief does neither. Every story is chosen because it changes an operator decision, and every story is written with operator context by an editor who has run the work.
Can I expense this?
Yes. Invoices and W9 are available on request. Most subscribers expense this under continuing education, industry intelligence, or executive subscriptions. We will send any documentation your AP team needs.
Do you take vendor sponsorship?
No. The Brief is subscriber funded and will remain subscriber funded. No vendor pays to be mentioned, and no vendor can pay to have a story omitted. The editor will take pitches for context on deals or trends, but every inclusion decision is editorial.
What if the Brief is not useful to me?
Full refund within the first 30 days, no questions. After that, cancel anytime and the subscription does not auto renew without your confirmation.

Email scott.lee@logicrove.com. Subscribers get a direct reply. Tip lines are prioritized for Principal tier.
